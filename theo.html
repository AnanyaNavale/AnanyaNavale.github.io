Theo

Gentle nudges. Real progress.

Timeline Fall 2025, 10 weeks
Role Lead Designer & Front-End Developer
Tools Figma, React Native (TypeScript), Supabase, Expo.dev
Class CS 147: Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction

Theo was my first real introduction to the HCI design process, and it became the project that made me fall in love with design. I took on a broad role – leading visual design, building a series of prototypes from low-fi paper through Figma med-fi, developing a functional hi-fi application, conducting needfinding interviews, and contributing to development. Each stage of the process taught me something new, and I trusted it.

The primary design experience I aimed to create within the product was simplicity. For users with ADHD, visual noise isn't just aesthetic – it's a barrier. I pushed for a clean, neutral interface that stayed out of the way, letting the experience feel passively supportive rather than stimulating. That conviction carried us to Best Visual Design at the CS 147 Fall 2025 Final Project Expo.
