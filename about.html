About Me

Hi, I'm Ananya – an undergraduate pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science at Stanford University, with focuses in Human-Computer Interaction and Systems.

I work at the intersection of design, engineering, & research, with a focus on accessibility and human-centered design. I care about building things that are thoughtful, functional, and made with the people using them in mind.

Outside of my coursework, I contribute writing and photography to The Stanford Daily through articles, photo galleries, and the occasional concert review.
